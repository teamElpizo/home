Unpacking the Problems

At first glance, it may seem as though the issue COVID-19 brings are the inconvenience of social distancing. However, when unpacking the issues, problems like affected personnel serving their stay home notice leads to roster gaps, which is a bane for services that run 24/7. Also, the virus lead to sudden changes in protocols, which are hard to adapt to given such a short amount of time as many of these SOPs were habitually drilled in training... As such, the one issue turns out to be a multifaceted problem, and the P=NP problem dictates that there cannot be a simple panacea to resolve such a complicated task.
